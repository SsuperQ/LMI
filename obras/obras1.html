Ya te dije adios, ahora como te olvido. !

There are affective relationships and people that set us on fire, as if they were embedded in our DNA and in the essence that defines us. Losing them creates a distressing and devastating void.

How to overcome the absence of who was vital to our love life? The premise is as follows: if you manage to detach yourself from your ex (or any impossible love that surrounds your life) properly, you can reinvent yourself as you please. Time helps, it's true, but you have to help. In this book you will find a practical guide that will help you overcome emotional loss and survive worthily. Reading it will not eliminate the pain you must feel to get ahead, but it will make you more understanding and bearable: it will transform you into useful suffering. Make the decision to release in a final way the emotional ballasts that do not let you grow and you will be surprised at what you are capable of. May your inner strength mark the passage of a definitive and forceful farewell.
